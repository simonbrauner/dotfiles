Laptop specific part of the configuration for Lenovo IdeaPad 5 14ALC05.

* PC speaker

Disabling the annoying and extremely loud [[https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/PC_speaker#Globally][beep noises]].

#+begin_src conf :tangle /sudo::/etc/modprobe.d/nobeep.conf
blacklist pcspkr
#+end_src

* Touchpad

Tapping the [[https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Touchpad_Synaptics#Configuration][touchpad]] works as a left mouse click, right button is in the top right corner.

#+begin_src conf :tangle /sudo::/etc/X11/xorg.conf.d/70-synaptics.conf
Section "InputClass"
    Identifier "touchpad"
    Driver "synaptics"
    MatchIsTouchpad "on"
        Option "TapButton1" "1"
        Option "RTCornerButton" "3"
EndSection
#+end_src
