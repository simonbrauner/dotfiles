* Basic configuration

** Files location

Disabling backup files and setting location for the automatically generated custom file.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(setq make-backup-files nil)

(setq custom-file "~/.emacs.d/custom.el")
#+end_src

** Package management

Preparing [[https://github.com/jwiegley/use-package][use-package]] to download all the other packages.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(require 'package)

(setq package-archives '(("melpa" . "https://melpa.org/packages/")
                         ("org"   . "https://orgmode.org/elpa/")
                         ("elpa"  . "https://elpa.gnu.org/packages/")))

(package-initialize)

(unless package-archive-contents
  (package-refresh-contents))

(unless (package-installed-p 'use-package)
  (package-install 'use-package))

(require 'use-package)
(setq use-package-always-ensure t)
#+end_src

* Face

** General UI configuration

Omitting the welcome page, disabling useless parts of UI and setting the distance between sides and text.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(setq inhibit-startup-message t)

(scroll-bar-mode -1)
(tool-bar-mode -1)
(tooltip-mode -1)
(menu-bar-mode -1)

(set-fringe-mode 5)
#+end_src

** Font

Setting [[https://github.com/tonsky/FiraCode][Fira Code]] (without ligatures) font with a proper size. Downloaded from =ttf-fira-code= Arch Linux package.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(set-face-attribute 'default nil
                    :font "Fira Code"
                    :height 115)
#+end_src

** Completion

*** Packages

Completion by [[https://github.com/minad/vertico][vertico]] and [[https://github.com/oantolin/orderless][orderless]], additional info from [[https://github.com/minad/marginalia][marginalia]].

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(use-package vertico
  :init
  (vertico-mode)
  (savehist-mode))

(use-package orderless
  :custom (completion-styles '(orderless)))

(use-package marginalia
  :init (marginalia-mode))
#+end_src

** Advice

[[https://github.com/justbur/emacs-which-key][Which-key]] and [[https://github.com/Wilfred/helpful][helpful]] to seek wisdom.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(use-package which-key
  :init (which-key-mode)
  :config (setq which-key-idle-delay 1))

(use-package helpful
  :bind (("C-h f"   . helpful-callable)
         ("C-h k"   . helpful-key)
         ("C-h v"   . helpful-variable)
         ("C-h C-h" . helpful-at-point)))
#+end_src

** Org mode

*** Basics

Updating [[https://orgmode.org/][org mode]], setting the indentation and color.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(use-package org
  :config
  (setq org-src-preserve-indentation 1)
  (setq org-startup-indented 1)
  (set-face-background 'org-block "#2e303c"))
#+end_src

*** Org tempo

Shortcuts for code blocks.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(with-eval-after-load 'org
  (require 'org-tempo)

  (add-to-list 'org-structure-template-alist '("co" . "src conf"))
  (add-to-list 'org-structure-template-alist '("el" . "src emacs-lisp"))
  (add-to-list 'org-structure-template-alist '("hs" . "src haskell"))
  (add-to-list 'org-structure-template-alist '("sh" . "src shell")))
#+end_src
